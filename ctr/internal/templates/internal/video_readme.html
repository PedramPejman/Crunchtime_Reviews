Video Posting Directions
Record your video using whatever means you'd like (either the DML video studio or your own setup if previously arranged)
Prepend the Crunchtime intro to it using Final Cut Pro (or any other video editing software you would like). You can get this snippet by going to this link
Upload the video to Youtube using the Crunchtime account. The credentials for our Youtube account is in your email. Search "Crunchtime Youtube credentials" in your email.
Once uploaded, record the URL of the video and URL of the picture to the video.
Now you're ready to make the posting on our site!
